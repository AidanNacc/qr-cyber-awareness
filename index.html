Why did you scan this?

You scanned an unlabelled QR code just to see what it was. This is known as "Quishing" (QR Code Phishing).

If this were a real attack, that loading screen could have been installing malware or stealing your login credentials.

It happens in real life

Attackers paste malicious stickers over real QR codes on:

  • Restaurant Menus
  • Parking Meters
  • Posters & Flyers
